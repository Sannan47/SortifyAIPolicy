Privacy Policy for Sortify AI

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Sortify AI operates the SortifyAI mobile application.

Information Collection and Use:

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA):

We use your device camera to capture images of waste items for AI-powered classification.
Photos taken are processed locally on your device to identify recyclable materials.
Camera images are not stored or transmitted to our servers without your explicit action.
Photo/Media Access (android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE):

We access your device's photo gallery to allow you to select existing images for waste classification.
You have full control over which images you share with the app.
Selected images are processed only for classification purposes and are not uploaded or stored externally.
Location Permission (android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):

We access your location to display nearby recycling centers and waste disposal facilities on the interactive map.
Location data is used only to provide relevant local information and is not stored, tracked, or shared with third parties.
You can use most app features without granting location access.
Internet Permission (android.permission.INTERNET):

Required for Firebase authentication and syncing your activity data across devices.
Used to load map data and display recycling center information.
Necessary for leaderboard functionality and real-time updates.
User Data Collection:
We collect and store the following information:

Email address for account authentication
Activity data: items scanned, points earned, game scores
Day streak and scanning statistics
Leaderboard rankings
Data Storage and Security:

All user data is stored securely using Firebase Realtime Database with industry-standard encryption.
Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication with secure protocols.
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
Third-Party Services:

Firebase (Google): For secure authentication, real-time database, and user data storage
Google Maps API: For displaying recycling center locations and navigation
Data Sharing:

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
Your scanning activity and game scores may be visible to other users through leaderboard features (displayed anonymously or with chosen display name).
Images you scan are processed locally and not shared unless you explicitly choose to do so.
Data Retention:

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active.
You can request account deletion by contacting us, after which all associated data will be permanently deleted.
Children's Privacy:
Our app is designed for general audiences including users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us.

Your Rights:
You have the right to:

Access your personal data
Request correction of inaccurate data
Request deletion of your account and data
Opt out of location services while still using other app features
Changes to This Privacy Policy:
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
