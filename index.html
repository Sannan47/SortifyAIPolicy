Privacy Policy for Sortify AI

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Sortify AI operates the SortifyAI mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information.

Information Collection and Use

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

We use your device camera to capture images of waste items for AI-powered classification. Images are processed locally on your device and are not stored or transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.

Photo / Media Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

The app may access your device’s photo gallery to allow you to select existing images for classification. You have full control over which images are selected. These images are used only for classification and are not uploaded or stored externally.

Location Permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

Location access is used to display nearby recycling centers and waste disposal facilities on the map. Location data is not stored, tracked, or shared with third parties. You may continue using most app features without granting location access.

Internet Permission (android.permission.INTERNET)

Internet access is required for:

  • Firebase authentication
  • Syncing user activity across devices
  • Displaying map data and recycling center information
  • Leaderboard and real-time updates

User Data We Collect

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Activity data (items scanned, points earned, game scores)
  • Day streak and scanning statistics
  • Leaderboard rankings

Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored using Firebase Realtime Database with industry-standard encryption. Authentication is managed through Firebase Authentication using secure protocols. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and secure data storage
  • Google Maps API: Displaying recycling center locations

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Leaderboard information may be visible to other users using anonymized or user-selected display names. Scanned images are not shared unless you explicitly choose to do so.

Data Retention

User data is retained while your account remains active. You may request account deletion at any time, after which all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Children’s Privacy

Sortify AI is designed for a general audience, including users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal data without consent, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disable location access while continuing to use other features

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
